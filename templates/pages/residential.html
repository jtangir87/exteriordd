Composite Asphalt Shingle

Composite asphalt shingle are the most popular roof found on more than 80% of all homes. Composite shingles use an organic or fiberglas base saturated with asphalt and the exposed surface is impregnated with small chips or reflective elements.

Shake

Shakes are made from thin, wedge-shaped pieces of natural wood. The most common wood shakes are cedar and yellow pine. Wood shake roofs are very visually appealing and have a longer lifespan than traditional asphalt shingles.
